Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and sugar.
  2. Add milk, egg, and melted butter. Stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry, then flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
